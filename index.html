Critical Habitats by Ecosystem

Authored by: Daniel Helton

This map shows classifications for ecosystems across the United States and the locations of areas deemed critical habitats for animal life by the U.S. Fish & Wildlife Service.

Ecosystem data comes from Ecoregions 2017. Data for critical habitats comes from the U.S. Fish & Wildlife Service. The map was created with QGIS.

This map uses the North America Albers Equal Area (EPSG:102008) coordinate reference system.

Temperate coniferous forests are the sites of many critical habitats on the west coast. (Credit: Conservation Institute)
